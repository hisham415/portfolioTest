Biography Overview.

Suhail's career as a Cloud Architect at egabi Solutions was a dynamic journey marked by collaboration with major industry players such as Huawei, AWS, RedHat, and Microsoft. Over three impactful years, he led the architecting of solutions for diverse clients, including the Egyptian Government, AWS, and Huawei. His dual certification as a Cloud Architect for both AWS and Huawei Cloud underscored his commitment to mastering the intricacies of these platforms.

During his tenure, Suhail showcased his prowess in backend development, mastering technologies like Dot Net Core and Flask. His projects seamlessly integrated innovation with robust functionality, while his expertise extended to a range of databases, including MS SQL, MySQL, MongoDB, and DynamoDB. Suhail's versatility shone through as he navigated different cloud ecosystems, becoming proficient in AWS, Azure, and Huawei Cloud.

Now positioned as the DevOps Lead at Motori, a promising UAE company owned by the government, Suhail continues to shape the technological landscape. His role involves steering Motori's DevOps strategy, aligning technology with business goals, and driving innovation. Suhail's transition underscores not only his technical acumen but also his leadership skills, serving as an inspiration for aspiring technologists in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Additionally, his foray into AI and Data Analytics rounds out his skill set, empowering him to leverage data-driven insights for strategic decision-making. This amalgamation of skills solidifies Suhail's position as a dynamic technologist capable of orchestrating end-to-end solutions across various technological domains.
